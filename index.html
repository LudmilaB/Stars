Fill Stars

Fill as many stars as you can

0

HOW TO PLAY: Drag a color disk onto its neighboring hollow star of same color, to fill the star and get a point. A new color disk will replace the old one, drag it to fill another hollow star if possible. When a diagonal of stars is filled, they will turn hollow. Try to get more points by filling more stars. When you fill 2 diagonals simultaneously you get bonus points.
